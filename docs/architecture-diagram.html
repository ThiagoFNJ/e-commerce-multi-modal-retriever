Rules & departures

  • →Shared dense encoder for product-text & reviews — commensurable scores; isolates review content from the encoder as a confound.
  • →Lexical stays lexical — BM25 sparse for exact terms, not a second dense encoder.
  • →RRF over summed scores — the reference summed incommensurable channel scores; RRF needs no calibration and no labels.
  • ◦Deferred: learned fusion (needs relevance labels → benchmark-bound); review aspect knobs — K, θ, polarity.
